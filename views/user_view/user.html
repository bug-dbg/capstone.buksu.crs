About the System

The system helps students make informed enrollment decisions by providing recommendations for selective and optional courses based on their skills, knowledge, and interests.

Personalized Course Recommendations

A recommender system designed for BukSU students to provide personalized reports filtering information for preferred course interests. This also serves as a guide and suggestion basis that narrows down possible course choices based on the analyzed data and chosen interests.
